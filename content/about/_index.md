*Shifting the frame of what's possible with data.*

Frameshift is a pioneering technology company at the forefront of the data intelligence revolution. Founded by a team of world-class engineers, data scientists, and visionaries, we're dedicated to transforming how organizations understand, process, and act on their data.

## Our Story

Frameshift was born from a simple observation: despite living in an age of unprecedented data abundance, most organizations struggle to extract meaningful insights from their information assets. Traditional analytics tools were built for a simpler time—when data was smaller, slower, and more structured. Today's reality demands a fundamental shift in approach.

Our founders, veterans of leading technology companies and academic institutions, recognized that the next generation of analytical tools needed to be built from the ground up with modern challenges in mind. They envisioned platforms that could handle massive scale, operate in real-time, and leverage artificial intelligence to democratize advanced analytics.

What started as a research project in a Silicon Valley garage has evolved into a comprehensive suite of cutting-edge data intelligence platforms trusted by Fortune 500 companies and innovative startups alike.

## Our Mission

We believe that every organization, regardless of size or industry, should have access to world-class data intelligence capabilities. Our mission is to democratize advanced analytics by creating powerful, intuitive platforms that transform complex data challenges into competitive advantages.

We're not just building software—we're architecting the future of human-data interaction. Our vision extends beyond traditional analytics to a world where data becomes a natural extension of human intelligence, enabling faster decisions, deeper insights, and more innovative solutions.
