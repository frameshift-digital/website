## Platform Overview

Our flagship platforms combine decades of research with practical engineering to deliver enterprise-grade analytics solutions that scale from startups to Fortune 500 companies.

### Core Platforms

**[Auspex: Predictive Intelligence Platform](/catalogue/auspex/)**
Transform historical data into actionable future insights with our advanced machine learning platform. Auspex specializes in forecasting, risk assessment, and behavioral prediction with industry-leading accuracy.

*Key Capabilities:*
- Time series forecasting
- Customer behavior prediction
- Risk modeling and scenario analysis
- Anomaly detection and early warning systems
- Automated machine learning workflows

**[Seer: Real-time Analytics Engine](/catalogue/seer/)**
Process millions of events per second with sub-millisecond latency. Seer enables instant decision-making through real-time data processing and complex event analysis.

*Key Capabilities:*
- Stream processing at massive scale
- Complex event processing
- Real-time dashboards and alerting
- Live segmentation and personalization
- Operational intelligence monitoring

## Integration Ecosystem

Our platforms are designed to work together, creating a comprehensive analytics ecosystem that grows with your needs.

Combine real-time insights from Seer with predictive models from Auspex to create powerful analytical workflows:

- **Hybrid analytics** combining historical patterns with live events
- **Unified alerting** across all analytical processes
